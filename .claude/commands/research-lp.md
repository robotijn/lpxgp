Research the LP (Limited Partner) institution: $ARGUMENTS

## Research Steps

1. **Web Search**: Find recent news, press releases, and investment announcements
2. **Profile Data**: Look for:
   - AUM (Assets Under Management)
   - Investment focus (strategies, geographies, fund sizes)
   - Recent fund commitments
   - Key decision makers (CIO, investment team)
   - ESG/DEI requirements
3. **Mandate Analysis**: Identify their investment mandate and preferences
4. **News & Sentiment**: Recent headlines, any red flags or opportunities

## Output Format

Provide a structured summary:

```
## LP Profile: [Name]

### Quick Facts
- Type: [Pension/Endowment/Family Office/etc.]
- AUM: $X billion
- HQ: [Location]
- Website: [URL]

### Investment Focus
- Strategies: [PE, VC, Real Estate, etc.]
- Geography: [Regions]
- Fund Size Range: $X-Y million

### Recent Activity
- [Recent commitments, news]

### Key Contacts
- [Names and roles if found]

### Fit Assessment
- Strengths: [Why they might be a good match]
- Concerns: [Potential barriers]
```

If LP name is not provided, ask for it.
